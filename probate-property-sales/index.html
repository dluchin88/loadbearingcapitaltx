How Probate Property Sales Work

In Texas, if a property passes through a will, the executor typically needs court authorization to sell it. Depending on the will language and probate situation, this can be an independent administration (faster, less court involvement) or dependent administration (more court oversight).

We work with executors and estate attorneys throughout this process. We're not in a hurry — we understand that probate has its own timeline and we can wait for the appropriate court authority before moving to closing.

What We Look for in Probate Deals

  • ✓ Executor or administrator has authority (or will have it soon)
  • ✓ We can get clear title through the title company
  • ✓ Property is in our service area
